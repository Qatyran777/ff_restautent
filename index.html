Our History
Like any other distinctive place, we have our own special history. The idea for the restaurant came to the founders unexpectedly. During a walk through the forest, the creator of our restaurant got stuck hundreds of kilometers away from the nearest settlement. Far away from civilization and communication, they had to set up a rudimentary life for a while, to find and cook their own food.
